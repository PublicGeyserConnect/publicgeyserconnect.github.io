Skywars Contest

Requirements

All entrants must:

- be of at least 13 years of age (per Discord ToS)

- have a copy of Minecraft Bedrock edition (this means Win10, mobile, or any console they can get to GeyserConnect.net with)

- display good sportsmanship

- have a Discord account

- be willing to be in a voice chat for each round (they don't need to talk though), as an admin will be running each round from Spectate


Signing Up

We are running sign-ups until September 28th. Please join our Discord Server and run the command "+apply tourney" in our #bot‑spam channel to sign up.

We will also take requests for what maps you would like to play on. We will only be using the free-for-all maps for play. Feel free to get on the server and at least spectate around the maps to see what maps you would like to play on. The map preferences will be considered, but may not be used based on the needs of the tournament.


Dates

Sign-ups: present - August 28th.

Tournament: September 4th, 12:00 (pm) UTC

Starting brackets will be published here and on our Discord by September 3rd.


Gameplay and Ranking

The tournament will be run using a round ranking elimination method. Total number of sign-ups will determine which maps will be used and how the brackets will be laid out. All rounds will be free-for-all, no team games. The server has maps that can allow up to 16 simultaneous players. We may be running multiple maps side by side if we have the staff and the numbers to warrant it.


Rounds layout will be as follows:

-  75 players total: 16/12 player rounds, top 5/4 players will advance to the next round

- 50 or more total players: 12 (maybe 16) player rounds, top 4 (5) advance

- 30 or more players: 8 player rounds, top 3 advance

- less than 30 players: 4 player rounds, top 2 advance

- 12 players or less: 1v1 rounds on a map of the two players' choice


 If fewer than 8 players sign up, the tournament will be canceled


Rewards

The top 3 players will receive a gift code for a legit copy of Minecraft: Java Edition that they may use to claim a premium copy of the game.


Accessing the Server

Players will connect to the server via joining either us.geyserconnect.net or eu.geyserconnect.net (or the DNS if on a console). Navigate to "Geyser Servers" on the menu, and select "GeyserConnect Minigames." This will place players into the minigames lobby, at which point they may either make their way to the Skywars portal, or open their compass and click the Diamond Sword. On the skywars server, the Diamond helmet selects "Play a game," leather helmet selects "Spectate a game," and under each are a Redstone Torch and a Comparator. The torch is the single-player (free-for-all) maps, and the comparator is the team games (which we aren't using for the tourney). The spectate mode can be used to view a map with no players on it.


General Rules/Expectations

- Players may (and are encouraged to) play on the server to practice for the tournament!

- If starting set of players are not available on time, they may forfeit being allowed to play.

- Players must be good sports

- Java players will not be allowed in this tournament

- Server will be whitelisted to tourney entrants only

- "God Bridging" will be allowed, as this is aimed at Bedrock players (it's normally discourged).

- This is a Java server, pay attention to the "cooldown squares" under crosshairs for attacks. High CPS doesn't help you here.

- This is a family-friendly environment (if you can't do/say it around your mom, don't do or say it here).

- Miscoduct of any kind may result in being barred from the tournament, regardless of your status.
