How to join the Public GeyserConnect on an Xbox or Nintendo Switch

Both of these consoles support the use of changing the DNS, which can be used for a trick that returns a counterfeit address to the "Featured Servers", that connects your device to the GeyserConnect instead of a featured server.
For this reason, we have an "Official Servers" group of servers that connect to the Featured Servers, so you don't have to undo the DNS settings.

  • STEP ONE: Change your DNS settings from Automatic to Manual

  • STEP TWO: Change the Primary DNS from 000.000.000.000 to 136.49.126.87

  • STEP THREE: Change the Secondary DNS from 000.000.000.000 to 136.49.126.87, 8.8.8.8, or 1.1.1.1


Finally, it is reccomended to restart your system for the changes to take effect. On an Xbox, it's reccomended to disconnect from your network, restart the Xbox, then reconnect to the network once the restart is completed.
