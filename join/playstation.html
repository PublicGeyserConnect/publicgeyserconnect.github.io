How to join the Public GeyserConnect on a Playstation

The methods below will use the IP "geyserconnect.net" along with port "19132".
Choose a method you can use to fake a LAN multiplayer game.

iOS DEVICES

Download BedrockTogether, enter in the server details, and open Minecraft to find a LAN game.

ANDROID DEVICES

Download GeyserMC's app, choose "LAN Proxy/Join a Bedrock Server", enter in the server details, and open Minecraft to find a LAN game.
If you're unable to install APK's, you may use MC Server Connector, but it has ads and in-app purchases unlike GeyserMC's app.

COMPUTERS

Download and setup Phantom on your computer. Also, you can install an Android emulator, such as BlueStacks.
Enter in the server details, and open Minecraft to find a LAN game.
